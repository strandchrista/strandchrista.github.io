Why Insite Perspective

Adoption in the built environment is difficult.

The sales channels in construction and development are rarely linear. Decisions are influenced by a complex network of owners, consultants, contractors, regulators, and investors — each with their own incentives and risk considerations. As a result, the industry is cautious and slow to adopt change.

Strong ideas often stall not because the innovation is weak, but because the path to credibility, buy-in, procurement, and implementation is unclear. The people evaluating a technology are often different from the people who ultimately approve, fund, and deliver the project.

Insite Perspective helps clients navigate this complexity. By mapping how decisions are actually made within the built environment, innovations can be positioned so they resonate with the stakeholders who control project outcomes. The result is a clearer path from technical potential to real industry adoption.
